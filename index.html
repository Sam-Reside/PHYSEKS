Welcome to PHYSEKS!

PHYSEKS is your interactive Physics practice platform, inspired by ALEKS. Use it to explore topics, practice exercises, and track your progress in a convenient pie chart overview.

How to Use This App

  • Sidebar Navigation: Expand a unit on the left to see its topics. Click a topic to reveal individual problems.
  • Working on Problems: Select a problem to display it in the main area. You can mark problems as complete, view solutions (if provided), or navigate between problems.
  • Assignments: Use the “Add Assignment” button in the header to group specific problems. Track each assignment’s progress via the progress bar and text.

Understanding the Pie Chart

The colored pie chart in the center of the screen shows your overall progress by unit. Each wedge represents one unit; the darker portion indicates completed topics, while the lighter portion shows those remaining.

Hover over any slice to see the number of completed versus total topics, along with a percentage. As you complete more problems, the wedges fill in and the center label updates accordingly.

Get Started

Open the sidebar, pick a topic, and start solving problems! Watch the pie chart fill up as you mark items complete.
